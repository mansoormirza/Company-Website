Our Services

CYBERSECURITY GOVERNANCE

We establish the architecture that ensures your company's programs alignment with business objectives, comply with regulations and standards (such as PCI security standards / GDPR / ISO / NIST) and achieve objectives for managing cybersecurity and risk

CYBERSECURITY RISK MANAGEMENT

We identify, analyze, evaluate, and address your organization's cybersecurity threats. This provides a snapshot of threats that might compromise your organization's cybersecurity and how severe they are. On the basis of this analysis, we conduct a thorough risk treatment process for you

CYBERSECURITY COMPLIANCE

We help you adhere to standards and regulatory requirements set forth by some law or an authority. Organizations must achieve compliance by establishing risk-based control that protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA) of the information

APPLICATION SECURITY

We implement application-level security measures to prevent data or code within the app from being compromised by including security during application development and design, as well as systems and approaches to protect apps after they are deployed

WEB SECURITY

Web security is critical to the smooth operation of any computer-based business. If a website is hacked or hackers gain access to your systems or software, your website—and even your entire network—can be brought down, effectively halting business operations

CLOUD SECURITY

We provide a comprehensive set of technology and best practices to protect cloud computing environments, cloud-running applications, and cloud-storage data. The full scope of cloud security is designed to protect the entire cloud infrastructure

VULNERABILITY ASSESSMENT & TESTING

Certain tools are used to detect, categorize, and score vulnerabilities in a system. In addition, we actively exploit the vulnerabilities to determine their severity, authenticity, potential for causing damage, and other insights

CYBERSECURITY MONITORING & DETECTION

We provide threat detection services and strategies that rely on automation to continuously scan your digital ecosystem for control flaws, frequently sending alerts to a security incident and event management system

CYBERSECURITY AWARENESS & TRAINING

As part of our core strategy to prevent and mitigate user risk, we offer cybersecurity training. These programs are intended to help users and employees understand their role in combating information security breaches.
